How payments works

Choose payment method You can choose your preferred currency to pay this invoice, perhaps based on the most convenient exchange rate. Scan QR Code Using your preferred wallet, scan the presented QR code which should fill the address and amount on your wallet. Network fees are up for your choosing. Wait few seconds Once the payment is seen on the mempool, it will appear on the invoice's screen. You don't need to refresh the page, although feel free to do so.
